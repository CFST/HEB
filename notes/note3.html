The Hotmail Header Reset

Hotmail replaces your header color styles with a green color on H2, H3, H4, H5, and H6 tags. In this example, the color is reset to black. Replace black with your choice of color. The !important is really what is overriding Hotmail's styling. A SIDE NOTE: Hotmail also sets the H1 and H2 tags to the same size.

.ExternalClass h1, .ExternalClass h2, .ExternalClass h2 a, .ExternalClass h2 a:visited, .ExternalClass h3, .ExternalClass h3 a, .ExternalClass h3 a:visited, .ExternalClass h4, .ExternalClass h5, .ExternalClass h6 { color: black !important; line-height: 100% !important; }
