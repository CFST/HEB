Gmail/Hotmail Image Display Fix

Gmail and Hotmail unwantedly adds in an extra space below images (typically seen when a viewer is using a non IE browser). This can be especially painful when you putting images on top of each other or putting back together an image you spliced for formatting reasons. Either way, you can add style="display: block;" to remove that space below the image.

<img src="image/yourimage.gif" alt="Your alt text" width="x" height="x" style="display:block;" />
