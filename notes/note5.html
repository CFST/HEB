Using a Wrapper/Container Table

Use a wrapper table to control the width, height and the background color of your email. Use this approach instead of setting attributes on the body tag or on a div tag. Add the #backgroundTable id to fix some issues with alignment and height in certain email clients.

<table cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" border="0" id="backgroundTable"> <tr> <td> ...content of email here (probably in another table :)... </td> </tr> </table>
